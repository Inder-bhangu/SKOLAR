Exploring the Beauty of Araku Valley

Araku Valley, located in the Eastern Ghats of India, is a picturesque hill station known for its stunning landscapes, coffee plantations, and rich tribal culture. Nestled in the state of Andhra Pradesh, Araku Valley offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

The valley is renowned for its pleasant climate, lush greenery, and serene environment. Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, including trekking, visiting coffee plantations, exploring tribal museums, and marveling at the beautiful waterfalls.
