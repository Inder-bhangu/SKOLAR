My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Shawshank Redemption

    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton
    • Brief Description: Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

  2. The Godfather

    • Year: 1972
    • Main Actors: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan
    • Brief Description: The aging patriarch of an organized crime dynasty transfers control of his clandestine empire to his reluctant son.

  3. The Dark Knight

    • Year: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
    • Brief Description: When the Joker wreaks havoc on Gotham City, Batman must use all his skills to stop him and restore order to the city.

  4. Inception

    • Year: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page
    • Brief Description: A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a CEO.

  5. The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

    • Year: 2001
    • Main Actors: Elijah Wood, Viggo Mortensen, Ian McKellen
    • Brief Description: In a small village in the Shire, a hobbit named Frodo Baggins inherits the One Ring from Bilbo Baggins and learns that it must be destroyed in the fires of Mount Doom.
